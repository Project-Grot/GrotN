Here is a brief history of our goals, work, and aspirations.

  1. We started in February, 2024

    We started this mission based on our own sufferings and observations of the Dutch housing market.

  2. We were funded in April, 2024

    After winning Nisbet Venture Fund pitch competition and being awarded $2,500, we are accelarating our development process.

  3. GrotN is launching in June, 2024

    With our launch, we are starting to embark on our mission with more than 280 of our early users accompanying us.

Our vision is simple.

Carefully matching tenants with each other aiming to increase the rate of tenants per apartment. While doing so, we are taking in the housing options of landlords who have been suffering from real estate agents or independent sales. Thus, these unseen houses in the market are matched with suitable tenants with high efficiency.

This efficiency provided by our algorithm is what will mitigate the housing crisis.
